About Us

Education360 is a final year college project built to provide a seamless digital reading experience for students, teachers, and book lovers.

Our Mission

To make learning accessible and convenient through a smart, responsive e-library that anyone can use anywhere, anytime.

Key Features

  • Online Book Reader
  • User Accounts & Registration

  • Responsive & Mobile Friendly
  • Category-wise Browsing
